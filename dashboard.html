Data visualization description

This dashboard aims at presenting the best, worst and average runner performance of each race group over year. Each time you move the mouse to a donut area or bar, more detailed information will be presented. Furthermore, you can use year slider situated at the middle of the dashboard to observe the variation of the male and female runner distribution for each race by donut chart and the fluctuation of the performance tendency for each race over year. If you are interested in observing the variation for a given category, for instance 42km-male, you can hover your mouse on the corresponding donut area or the relevant bar in no matter which bar chart to highlight the same category in all the 4 charts at the same time.
