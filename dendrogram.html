Data visualization description

Initially, the purpose of this dendrogram is to give user a brief perception about the structure of Lausanne marathon competition since the structure and the way they computed the final rank is not intuitionistic and more or less complicated. From our point of view, using dendrogram is the most efficient way to present the competition structure. Moreover, we add a histogram at the end of each last leaf in order to show the number of participants of each race, gender and age group so as to present the runner distribution of the competition. We add a time slider at the bottom of the graph so that user can observe the distribution variation over year and find some interesting tendency and insights.
